Her Story

Started with obedience,
not perfection

There was a time I was opening up my home with no furniture, no real setup — not even all the things I felt like I "needed" to look credible. Just obedience. Just vision. Just faith.

What I had in the beginning may not have looked like much to other people, but it was enough for God to work with. I gave Him my yes, and He's been watering the vision ever since.

Restoring Eve Community was born from that place — a space where women stop carrying invisible weights alone, and start walking out lighter, together.
